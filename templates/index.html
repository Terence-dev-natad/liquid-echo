About Liquid Echo

Team Liquid Echo, a powerhouse in the Mobile Legends: Bang Bang esports scene, emerged from a strategic partnership between Team Liquid and the renowned AURA Esports. The team's formation represents a blend of expertise and competitive spirit, bringing together top-tier talent from the Philippines to dominate the Mobile Legends battlefield. With a roster that includes celebrated players like Benedict "BennyQt" Gonzales and Karl "KarlTzy" Nepomuceno, Team Liquid Echo has quickly made a name for itself, securing a World Championship and aiming for continued success on the global stage.

Team Liquid Echo's journey is marked by resilience and strategic prowess. In their debut season, they faced formidable opponents, showcasing their skill and determination by advancing through challenging playoff brackets. Despite early setbacks, the team consistently demonstrated their potential, culminating in their triumph at the MLBB M4 World Championships. This victory not only highlighted their tactical superiority but also their ability to perform under pressure, earning them recognition as one of the premier teams in the Mobile Legends esports arena.

Beyond their in-game achievements, Team Liquid Echo has cultivated a strong fanbase, particularly in the Philippines, where their matches draw significant attention and support. The team's success is a testament to their rigorous training, strategic gameplay, and the synergy between their players. As they continue to compete in major tournaments, Team Liquid Echo remains committed to pushing the boundaries of what is possible in Mobile Legends esports, aiming for even greater heights and more championship titles.

KarlTzy, born on August 22, 2004, began his career in Season 2 with Finesse Solid and later joined SGD Omega. He represented the Philippines in the 30th Southeast Asian Games, winning gold. After a stint with Bren Esports, where he won the M2 World Championship, KarlTzy moved to ECHO and secured his second world championship at M4, earning the title "The Young Goat." He is the first two-time M World Championship winner. His nickname is inspired by Dota 2 player Arteezy, and his success has influenced other players to adopt the "-Tzy" suffix.

Sanford Marin Vinuya, known as "Sanford," is a prominent EXP laner for Team Liquid Echo in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang. Born on June 24, 2006, he began his professional career with Nexplay EVOS in June 2021 and joined ECHO in June 2022. Sanford's achievements include winning the M4 World Championship and earning the Finals MVP title in MPL PH Season 13​​. His gameplay is characterized by strategic prowess and mechanical skill, notably with his favorite hero, Yu Zhong. Beyond esports, Sanford plans to invest in real estate and start a coffee shop, reflecting his future aspirations​
